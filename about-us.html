About us

We are a local Swedish resturant located in the heart of Vancouver British Columbia! We arrived in this land, not only to experiance life here, but to share with all of you what our passion is as well!

Our Mission

Our mission is a simple one: To share with you the food of our people! We thought about opening a resturant like this, because of a severe lack of Swedish food in the city.

"We will always strive to produce the very best Sweden has to offer!" -- Karl

The Team

Karl Stiernsköld

Owner

Karl is the owner and founder Skål, and a firm beleiver of freash and organic food. While always striving for the best, he makes sure that the prices of all the dishes are affordable without compromising on quality.

Freja Burensköld

Head Chef

Since She was young Freja has always loved to cook. Today, she still has that same passion and fire for Swedish food, and she is more than exited to share it with the world.

Oliver

He may not look it but Oliver has one of the biggest sweettooths any man his age can have, and makes sure that his pastries are the best of them all.

Ella

Need a quick drink? Then Ella's got you covered! No matter how complex or bizarre you may want it to be, she will be more than happy to experiment and give it a try.
